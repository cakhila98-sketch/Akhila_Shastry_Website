Reiki
Energy Healing · Level 2 Practitioner

Reiki is a Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that also promotes healing. As a Level 2 practitioner, I offer distance sessions that begin with a conversation — I want to understand what you're carrying before we begin. Sessions are 45 minutes and conducted online. Many people leave feeling lighter, more grounded, and clearer than when they came in.

Chakra Meditation
Inner Balance · Daily Practice

The chakra system maps the body's seven energy centres — from root to crown. Through guided meditation and breathwork, I work with these centres daily to maintain emotional and energetic balance. It is a practice of coming home to the body, of learning to listen to what it is already telling you. Slow, intentional, and deeply grounding.

Vipassana
Insight Meditation · 10-Day Retreat · 2024

In 2024, I sat a 10-day Vipassana silent meditation retreat. No phone, no speaking, no distractions — just the mind and its contents. It was one of the most challenging and clarifying experiences of my life. The technique, as taught by S.N. Goenka, is simple: observe reality as it is, not as you want it to be. I returned with a daily practice that has not left me since.
